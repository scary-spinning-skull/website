Bestiary
Here are some examples of the kinds of opponents a character may encounter, and, if they are lucky, defeat.

This is far from an exhaustive list, but it should give you a sense of how enemies in this game are "statted" and provide you with a framework to come out with opponents on the fly. Enemies may be natural, intelligent, and even science-fictional. It was mad science (funded by a mid-tier state university) that birthed this society in the first place, so characters shouldn't be surprised to find themselves being pursued by cyborgs.

Additionally, some of the opponents encountered may have special characteristics which will affect their behavior and limit the number of places in which they appear. Those abbreviations are also listed below; DMs are, of course, free to add or remove whatever they want.

Also bear in mind that some of these enemies may be armed - they are intelligent, after all! It is the job of the DM to decide what, if any, special properties they have.

  • AG = Aboveground Only
  • UG = Underground Only
  • I = Intelligent
  • FS = Full success to hit only
  • CO = Cowardly, doesn't like fighting
  • FL = Flies
  • S = Slow
  • FA = Fast
  • F = Fighter
  • C = Caster
  • D = Dodger
  • O = Orator
  • S = Scalawag
  • AD = Advantaged
