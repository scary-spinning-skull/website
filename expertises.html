Expertises
Expertises are divided into three classes: Martial, Academic, and Professional. Players who are creating a character must choose one of each to start with. As the character gains XP, they may choose to spend it on developing expertise in various fields, an activity which takes up a month of game time.

Players may choose these freely, but they are numbered for those who are brave enough to let the dice decide.


Martial
  1. Archer - use of a bow.
  2. Assassin - striking from cover.
  3. Bodyguard - protecting a specific individual from harm.
  4. Bounty Hunter - tracking fugitives.
  5. Gladiator - fighting in front of a crowd.
  6. Knight - jousting and chivalry.
  7. Swordsman - bladed weapons.
  8. Warrior - handheld non-bladed weapons.
  9. Wizard - using spells to make enemies fight.
  10. Wrestler - paw-to-paw combat.

Academic
  1. Alienist - psychology.
  2. Cartographer - making and reading maps.
  3. Engineer - the construction of buildings and tunnels.
  4. Historian - the history of the Substreet.
  5. Humanist - human behavior.
  6. Lawyer - Substreet law and customs.
  7. Librarian - knowledge of books and writing.
  8. Linguist - spoken languages.
  9. Orator - public speaking.
  10. Physician - healing and medicine.

Professional
  1. Alchemist - mixing potions.
  2. Armorer - crafting armor.
  3. Gambler - playing games of skill.
  4. Navigator - piloting watercraft.
  5. Jester - performing physical comedy.
  6. Merchant - buying and selling goods.
  7. Minstrel - playing musical instruments and singing.
  8. Scavenger - finding items to craft.
  9. Scout - living in the wild.
  10. Spy - clandestine surveillance and extracting information.
