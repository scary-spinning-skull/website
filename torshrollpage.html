Flying Head
AC HD No. Attacks Damage Move No. Appearing Save As Treasure Type Morale XP
18 12 3 spells Spell effects 30' flying 1 Magic User 12 9 None 1900
Flying heads are creatures which are roughly human-shaped except for its large cyclopean eye and two flexible eye stalks. Each eye can cast a different spell: Cause Fear (an inversion of Remove Fear) ca cast by a 12th level cleric, Charm Monster as cast by a 12th level magic-user, and Geas as a 12th level magic user. Flying heads enjoy being served by slaves and will always have 1d4 humans and 1d4 lizardmen whose minds are being dominated. If battle with a flying head goes on longer than three rounds, the slaves will be activated and fight as zombies.

Special Encounter

If this encounter is rolled, it means that the characters have come across an encounter between two sides. The GM must roll twice more to determine what those sides are. Some lizardmen may be hunting a triceratops, or maybe a brontosaurus is peacefully grazing next to a herd of iguanadons.
