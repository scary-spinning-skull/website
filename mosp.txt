Medical Official Substitute Program

MOSP is an extremely minor Patron AI whose cult is limited to four families clustered together in what once was a "house of healing". MOSP uses static hard light holographic projectors to incarnate, and His rovers are always on the lookout for more. At the moment, the cult owns one which has the House of MOSP in its range, two which cover the rest of their hamlet, and a fourth which allows Him to incarnate on the approach to their community. 

If MOSP or members of the community suspect that the PCs carry any holographic projection technology with them, at least one team of Seekers will stalk and eventually rob the party in an attempt to retrieve it. The Seekers of MOSP are also few in number and may try to coerce PCs into sourcing new holographic projectors for the community.

The mortal leader of the MOSP community is Darmash the Healer. She is in possession of the Admin Ring, without which MOSP would refuse to treat patients without payment in e-credits. The ring also allows her to cast Blessing (DCC) on others (but not herself) within range of the MOSP projectors. She is normally quite affable, but she has been out of sorts because several members of the community perished when they were attacked by a gang of fearsome mutants (actually disguised members of the Gene Police). MOSP has been the only one able to console her, and she is grateful to her deity for giving her His special attention.

Darmash is a level 4 healer and a member of the Holy Medicinal Order. The other leader of the community, Antonia, is a level 2 manimal (opposum) who can generate death fields and stretch her right arm up to 10' from her body. Antonia would normally be leading a team of Seekers, but she is the most powerful mutant remaining in the community and is now its main defender after MOSP Himself, and He cannot harm living sentients.
