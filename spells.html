SPELL DESCRIPTIONS


Buzz

This spell causes the target to hear loud buzzing noises for two turns or five minutes, as if there were several large flies hovering hear their ear. This noise is very distracting, and all of the target's actions are Hindered for its duration. A failure causes the target to hear the voices of their friends cheering them on, and their next action is Emboldened.


Explode Head

This spell causes the target's head to explode with the force of one stick of dynamite. Any characters or other creatures near the target are also harmed as is normal for such an explosion. A failure causes the caster to explode with the same force. Casting this spell requires the expenditure of six Soul points and reduces their HP to 1.


Forget Ensorcellment

The target of this spell forgets that he had ever been affected by a spell of any kind. He will rationalize its effects and become confused and irate if those rationalizations are interrogated. This is a very important spell to use in non-combat situations where the target may be on your side. Failure causes the caster to forget one randomly selected spell for one month.


Gigachad

The target of this spell becomes more respected as he develops a strong jaw, chin, and cheekbones. The fur on their head grows longer and thicker and looks as if it has been combed back. Salamanders grow a head of hair which behaves in the same way. The spell lasts for 24 hours, and a failure causes the caster to seem weak and uninteresting to others for two weeks.

On The Wall

Casting this spell causes magical writing of up to two square yards to appear on any vertical surface for ten minutes. The caster can make text appear between 1'' high and 3'. If the caster is illiterate, the "text" resembles vague scribblings. A failure causes the text to tell its reader exactly where the caster is hiding and how to catch them.


Understand Languages

The target of this spell can understand any language spoken within earshot for ten minutes. They are unable to speak or read the languages, but the verbal utterances are clear. If there is a failure, the caster will forget all speech for the same duration and be unable to cast spells for that span.
