SPELL DESCRIPTIONS


Believe Lie

The target of this spell will believe any lie, but not a direct command, told to them by the caster in the next fifteen seconds. The spell lasts for a full minute, after which they will come to their senses and realize that the lie is untrue. They will also remember who told them the lie. The target will otherwise behave normally while under this spell.

Betterize

This spell embetters the target, causing their next action to be Emboldened, even if the action will be Emboldened by some other effect. The caster cannot use this spell on himself, and the target of this spell cannot be enbettered again for 24 hours.

Buzz

This spell causes the target to hear the sound of several large flies buzzing hear their ears for up to five minutes. This noise is very distracting, and as a result all of the target's actions are Hindered for its duration.

Distraction

The target of this spell will be distracted by something which may or may not be real for up to a minute. While under the influence of this spell, the target may still look at the caster, but they will not act against them.

For example, Scabbers has once again tried to cast a spell, this time successfully. The target, a guard, becomes consumed by the delusion that his commander has been stealing cheese from his locker and he begins honing his sword in anticipation of confronting him about it later. Scabbers now has a full minute to escape from the cell before his guard remembers that he does not keep cheese in his locker.

ESP

Only the caster may be affected by this spell, which enables him to magically sense one thing up to 10' away for up to a full minute, during which time they are still able to perform Hindered acts. This thing may have happened up to a day in the past or up to a minute in the future, and the spell allows them to use only one sense. The sense used will be highly specific, so the caster may detect proprioreception or balance in a target area as if they were in that space, and they also access senses which they do not normally possess, such as magnetoreception.

Explode Head

This spell causes the target's head to explode with the force of one stick of dynamite. No one near the target is harmed by the explosion, although they can see and hear it. A failure causes the caster to explode with the same force. Casting this spell requires the expenditure of six Soul points and reduces the caster's HP to 1.

Forget Ensorcellment

The target of this spell is made unaware that he has been affected by a spell of any kind. He will rationalize the effects of the spell itself in any way he can, becoming confused and irate if those rationalizations are questioned. This is a very important spell to use in non-combat situations where the target may be on your side.

For example, a mouse is half starved after being hit with a super effective No Opening spell and spending a day in a small closet. When the spell fades and he stumbles out, he decides that he must have forgotten to look in the door's direction while searching for an exit.

Gigachad

The target of this spell becomes more respected as he develops a strong jaw, cheekbones, and a human-like chin. The fur on their head grows longer and thicker and looks as if it has been combed back. Salamanders grow a head of hair which behaves in the same way. The spell lasts for 24 hours.

Gross

This spell causes the target to percieve the caster as something which would be revolting if eaten. This spell is only useful for carnivorous opponents which may be inclined to eat the caster, but it will still effect enemies which do not intend to eat rats or mice, such as Giants or pigeons.

Hallucination

This spell creates an auditory and visual delusion that the target believes is absolutely real. Even attempts to interact with the hallucination will "feel" real for the duration of the spell. The hallucination is not perceptible to others.

Heal Wounds

This spell completely restores a target's lost HP. This does not work on targets who have already died, but may possibly be used in conjunction with Prevent Death.

Illusion

Creates a realistic visual apparition. This can be anything: a solid wall, a Giant striding around the corner of a sewer, and so on. The illusion can be seen by anyone in the area, but it vanishes the moment that anyone attempts to touch it. If it is not interfered with, the illusion lasts for ten minutes.

No Opening

This spell imposes a delusion on the target that there is no way to leave the room they are in. Doors, sewer grates, and even cracks in a wall are not visible to them. Targets who attempt to break out by, say, smashing a hole in the wall will remain trapped because they will be unable to see the hole once it has been made. The spell lasts for one hour, and if they are forcibly removed before then they will be so bewildered that any action they take will be double Hindered.

Prevent Death

Casters can only use this spell if someone else, be they PC, NPC, or even an animal, has just been killed. The target's body will heal enough to allow them to be brought to 1 HP if aid is administered quickly; otherwise the target dies permanently. This spell will not work if the body is not intact, such as after being dissolved in acid or torn in half by an opposum. Targets who have been dead for more than a minute will not be revived.

Puppeteer

The caster of this spell is able to control the actions of the target as if they were a puppet for one hour. They must make "flapping hand" gestures and talk out of the corner of their mouth while forcing the target to speak, and they must mime controlling a marionette to make the target walk around and perform actions. All actions performed by the target of this spell are double Hindered as people under this spell's control can only move jerkily and are being guided by someone who is possibly far away. Intelligent creatures of any species may notice the strange movements and the fact that the victim's voice is the same as that of the caster.

Reverse Handedness

The target of this spell has their handedness reversed for an hour: a left-handed target becomes right-handed and vice versa. A target in combat suddenly becomes clumsy as he is now holding his weapon in the wrong hand. He must spend a turn switching hands to use his weapons "correctly" or be Hindered as he fights.

Sleepy

Targets become sleepy for fifteen seconds. If they are performing an action, it will now be Hindered. If the target is already prone or in a relaxed pose, they will fall into a natural sleep from which they can be woken by a loud noise or being shaken by someone. br>

Super Heal

The target can be brought to one HP above full health, and their next action is Emboldened. The extra HP cannot be recovered by ordinary healing - once it's lost, it's lost.

Throw Voice

This spell allows the caster to project his voice to any point within his line of sight. This spell is limited to sounds that the caster can produce vocally, from a loud shout to a quiet whisper. The caster cannot throw his voice in a language he doesn't know unless he is mimicking something he has memorized.

On The Wall

Casting this spell causes text to appear on any vertical surface for up to ten minutes. The text can be between 1'' and 1' high and fill a space of up to one square yard. If the caster is illiterate, the "text" resembles asemic writing.

Understand Languages

The target of this spell can understand any spoken language for ten minutes. They cannot speak it themselves or read it, though, even if they are literate in other languages.

Night Vision

The caster can give himself the ability to see in black and white, even in absolute darkness. Looking at lit spaces while under the effect of this spell will not blind the caster.
