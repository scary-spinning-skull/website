Equipment

The denizens of the Substreet use many tools and pieces of equipment, especially when transiting through areas with potential hostiles where aid is most remote.

Potions

Herbal Potions
Herbal potions are easy to source from Human centers of commerce. There are large racks in a variety of gas stations and convenience stores which have a bewilderingly vast variety of tablets in easy-to-reack packaging. The difficult is finding the right kind - the names are constantly changing, and the packaging is a confusing kaleidoscope of colors and images of plants and ferocious animals, sometimes printed on boxes with images that shift as the viewer changes position. It takes a competent Humanist to be able to figure out which is the right product from that variety.
Human Potions
The most potent potions made specifically for Humans are far less confusing. Either a literate Humanist or a Physician can make the right choice when choosing from several options, and preparing them for Animal consumption without a Chemist is extremely dangerous.
